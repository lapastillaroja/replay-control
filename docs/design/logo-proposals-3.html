BATCH 3

Replay Control — Logo Proposals

Twelve logo concepts exploring game cartridges, anime-style characters, and the playful intersection of both. Companion app for RePlayOS on Raspberry Pi.

View Batch 1 (concepts 01-10) View Batch 2 (concepts 11-23)
THEME A

Game Cartridges

The iconic shape of game cartridges reimagined as bold logo marks.

24

Cartridge Shield

A game cartridge shape merged with a heraldic shield silhouette. The cartridge pins become the shield's base, and a play triangle sits in the label area like a coat of arms. Protection, legacy, and the sacred nature of your game collection.

25

Cartridge Stack

Three cartridges stacked at a slight fan angle, like a hand of cards. Represents a library, a collection, the joy of choosing which game to play next. Each cartridge has a different subtle detail on its label.

26

Glowing Cartridge

A front-facing cartridge radiating energy lines and sparkle particles. The cartridge is alive with power, glowing from within like it contains something magical. The label area pulses with light.

27

Bite-Mark Cartridge

A cartridge with a playful pixel-art bite taken out of one corner, like a cookie or the Apple logo. Irreverent, fun, memorable. Suggests the cartridge is so good you could eat it. A cheeky nod to blowing on cartridges.

THEME B

Anime-Style Characters

Cute mascot characters that could become the face of Replay Control.

28

Chibi Gamer

A chibi-style character with oversized headphones and starry eyes, rendered in pixel art. The headphones have a small antenna, and the character flashes a peace sign. Friendly, approachable, unmistakably gaming-focused.

29

Pixel Cat

A cute pixel-art cat with pointed ears and a curled tail, sitting attentively. The cat's eyes are bright and playful, with a tiny pixel collar. Cats and gaming go together like save points and relief.

30

Robot Buddy

A friendly retro robot with a CRT-screen face showing a play symbol, boxy pixel-art body, and antenna on top. Part R.O.B., part helper -- the perfect companion for managing your retro game library.

31

Pixel Warrior

A tiny pixel-art hero character in a classic adventure pose with a small sword raised high. Think the protagonist of every 8-bit RPG. Heroic, nostalgic, full of determination. The champion of your game library.

THEME C

Characters + Cartridges

The best of both worlds: mascot characters interacting with game cartridges.

32

Cartridge Rider

A chibi character riding a game cartridge like a surfboard or skateboard, with motion lines and sparkles trailing behind. Adventure, speed, fun. The cartridge becomes a vehicle for play.

33

Cartridge Cat

A playful cat peeking out from the top of a game cartridge, ears and paws visible above the label area. Like a cat in a box, but the box is a cartridge. Curiosity, surprise, delight.

34

Robot Loader

A small robot holding up a game cartridge, about to insert it. The robot's screen-face shows a happy expression. Helpful, purposeful, the perfect metaphor for a companion app that manages your games.

35

Cartridge Burst

A pixel warrior bursting upward out of a cartridge with an explosion of pixel particles, arms raised in triumph. The game comes to life. Energy, excitement, the magic moment when you press start.
