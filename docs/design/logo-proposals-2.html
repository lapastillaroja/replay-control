Replay Control — Logo Proposals

Thirteen more logo concepts exploring the joy, nostalgia, and tactile memories of retro gaming. Companion app for RePlayOS on Raspberry Pi.

View Batch 1 (concepts 01-10) View Batch 3 (concepts 24-35)
11

Power On

A pixelated power button symbol (circle with line) with spark/electricity effects. The moment of turning on the console — pure anticipation and excitement.

12

Cartridge

A minimalist side-view silhouette of a game cartridge being inserted — half in, half out. The tactile satisfaction of clicking a cartridge in. Clean geometric shape.

13

1UP

The iconic "1UP" text from classic games, stylized as a logo mark with pixel blocks and a small mushroom accent. Getting another chance — the essence of "replay."

14

Cassette Tape

A retro cassette tape shape with a play symbol on the label area. Nostalgia crossover between music and gaming culture of the 80s/90s. The mixtape of your favourite games.

15

TV Antenna

Old-school rabbit ear antenna on top of a minimal CRT TV shape. The simple joy of sitting in front of the TV to play. Very evocative, minimal lines.

16

Pixel Ghost

A friendly Pac-Man-style ghost rendered in pixel art with "RC" letter eyes. Cute, recognizable, universally associated with retro gaming joy.

17

Checkpoint Flag

A racing checkpoint flag in pixel-art style, waving on a pole. Represents progress, milestones, the satisfaction of reaching a save point.

18

D-Pad Rose

A D-pad (directional pad) shape reimagined as a compass rose with decorative points. Geometric, clean, represents navigation and control without being a full gamepad.

19

Pixel Explosion

An expanding burst of pixels, like defeating a boss or completing a level. Celebration, triumph, joy. A radial pattern of small squares shooting outward.

20

Trophy / High Score

A pixel-art trophy cup with a star on it. The universal symbol of achievement and the pride of earning a high score. Simple, celebratory silhouette.

21

Floppy Disk / Save Icon

The universal save icon reimagined with pixel art edges and a play symbol on the label. Nostalgic computing meets gaming — save your progress, replay your favourites.

22

Lightning Bolt

A pixel-art zigzag lightning bolt like a power-up. Energy, speed, excitement. Simple silhouette that works at any size with strong diagonal energy.

23

Replay Arrow

A circular replay/refresh arrow (like a ↻ symbol) with a small play triangle in the center. The definitive "replay" icon — clean, universally understood, and perfectly encapsulates both the concept of replaying and the play action. The circular arrow is in the accent color, the play triangle in white.
