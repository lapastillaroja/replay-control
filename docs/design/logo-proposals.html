Replay Control — Logo Proposals

Companion app for RePlayOS, a retro gaming OS for Raspberry Pi. Ten logo concepts exploring the joy and nostalgia of retro gaming.

01

Press Start Pixel Text

The letters "RC" rendered in chunky pixel-art style, evoking the "PRESS START" text burned into every arcade kid's memory. Blocky, bold, instantly retro.

02

Coin Slot

A stylized coin mid-drop into a slot, with motion lines. References the ritual "INSERT COIN" moment. Simple, iconic silhouette that works at any size.

03

CRT Glow

A rounded rectangle evoking a CRT screen with scan lines and a warm glow. A play/replay symbol sits inside. Captures the nostalgic warmth of a glowing monitor.

04

Replay Loop

A circular arrow formed by a trail of pixel squares, like a character leaving a pixel trail as it loops. Merges the "replay" concept with pixel-art aesthetics.

05

Arcade Button

Top-down view of a single illuminated arcade button with a glow ring. Bold and satisfying. An "R" sits inside, glowing with energy.

06

Star Power

A pixel-art 5-pointed star referencing power-ups and achievements. Contains a play symbol at its center. Universally recognized, radiates positive energy.

07

Warp Pipe / Portal

A circular portal with concentric rings suggesting depth, like entering a game world. The center holds a play symbol. Suggests discovery and adventure.

08

Joystick Spark

Just the abstract ball-top of a joystick with spark/star effects radiating around it. Not a full gamepad -- just the essence of energy and tactile excitement.

09

Heart Container

A pixel-art heart like the iconic heart containers from adventure games. Universal symbol of love and joy combined with retro gaming. Contains a play triangle.

10

Level Up Arrow

An upward arrow built from pixel-art steps, like climbing levels. Dynamic, forward momentum. Combined with a circular replay curve for the full concept.
